numpy.polynomial.laguerre.Laguerre.roots¶

Laguerre.roots()¶

Return list of roots.

Return ndarray of roots for this series. See lagroots for full documentation. Note that the accuracy of the roots is likely to decrease the further outside the domain they lie.
