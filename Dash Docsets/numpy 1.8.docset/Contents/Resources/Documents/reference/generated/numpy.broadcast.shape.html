numpy.broadcast.shape¶

broadcast.shape¶

Shape of broadcasted result.

Examples

>>> x = np.array([1, 2, 3])
>>> y = np.array([[4], [5], [6]])
>>> b = np.broadcast(x, y)
>>> b.shape
(3, 3)
