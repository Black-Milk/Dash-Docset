scipy.special.btdtr¶

scipy.special.btdtr(x1, x2, x3[, out]) = <ufunc 'btdtr'>¶

y=btdtr(a,b,x) returns the area from zero to x under the beta density function: gamma(a+b)/(gamma(a)*gamma(b)))*integral(t**(a-1) (1-t)**(b-1), t=0..x). SEE ALSO betainc
